A little about myself.

Hello world, I'm Ashley Downs. I am passionate about improving design for others. I'm constantly learning new things everyday. I love meeting new people who are motivated and unafraid of sharing ideas with the world.

I'm a developer, researcher and creative problem-solver. I have learned a lot of the processes, concepts, and theories of UX/UI and product design through workshops, reading blogs, and applying these to projects that I have completed as a developer. I like to say I am a big-picture kind of thinker. I enjoy working on products end to end, seeing the ideation all the way down to the final product.

During my undergrad, I attended Eastern Michigan University and studied Computer Science and English. I am well-versed in CSS, HTML, Java, and JavaScript. I have experience as a full-stack developer at a consultant firm in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

Outside of coding, I enjoy - painting, writing, and anything I can express myself with.
